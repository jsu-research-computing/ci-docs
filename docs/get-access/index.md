# Get Access

## Who can use JSU Research Computing?
Use is available only to current **JSU students, faculty, or staff**.

## Access process (summary)
1. Complete the Research Computing Intake Form
2. Attend an Intake Facilitation Meeting (about 1 hour)
3. Complete onboarding training (as needed)
4. Receive credentials and begin running workloads

## What to prepare for your intake meeting
- CPUs/GPUs requested
- memory/RAM needed
- expected data transfer size
- storage requirements
- short description of your research project
