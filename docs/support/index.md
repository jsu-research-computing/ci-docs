# Support

JSU Research Computing is supported by the **Cyberinfrastructure (CI) Facilitation Team**.  
We help students, faculty, and research teams plan computing needs, learn workflows, and troubleshoot issues.

---

## How to Get Help

When you contact us, please include:

- Your **name** and **JSU affiliation** (student/faculty/staff + department)
- A short **project description**
- What you were trying to do (1â€“2 sentences)
- Any **error message/output** (copy/paste text, remove sensitive data)
- The name of any related repo/page (if applicable)

This helps us respond faster and route your request correctly.

---

## Common Support Topics

We can help with:

- choosing the right resource (HTC vs. HPC vs. AI workflows)
- estimating CPU/GPU, memory, storage, and job counts
- HTCondor submit files and debugging job failures
- data transfer and file organization
- software environments (Python/Conda/modules/containers)
- onboarding and training recommendations

---

## Before You Email (Quick Checks)

Try these first if you can:

- confirm you are following the **Policies** page
- verify your submit file paths and filenames
- check output/error/log files generated by your job
- reduce to a small test job before submitting many jobs

If you believe you accidentally uploaded sensitive data or notice a security concern:
- Do **not** post details publicly
- Contact the CI team immediately through official channels
